Shomal University Forums

Shomal University was founded in 1995 and is the biggest non-governmental university in Iran, fully accredited by the Ministry of Science, Research, and Technology.
They needed a system to provide GIS and RS Forums to over 7000 students in 45 different courses at under-graduate and graduate levels.
Since I previously ran penetration tests on their domains and consulted them on how to improve their security on internet, I was entrusted to configure their server and run a forums management system with a customized design on their respective subdomain.
The project was done in 2013.
